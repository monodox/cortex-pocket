# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

* Using welcoming and inclusive language
* Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
* Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
* Focusing on what is best for the community
* Showing empathy towards other community members
* Promoting responsible AI development and usage
* Respecting user privacy and data protection
* Contributing to ethical AI practices

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
* Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
* Public or private harassment
* Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

## AI Ethics and Responsible Development

As an AI-powered application, we are committed to:

### Privacy First
* **Local processing by default**: Protecting user data through on-device computation
* **Transparent data handling**: Clear disclosure of any data transmission
* **User consent**: Explicit permission for any cloud-based features
* **Data minimization**: Collecting only necessary information

### Responsible AI Usage
* **Bias awareness**: Acknowledging and addressing potential model biases
* **Harmful content prevention**: Implementing safeguards against misuse
* **Transparency**: Clear communication about AI capabilities and limitations
* **User empowerment**: Providing users control over AI interactions

### Open Source Commitment
* **Transparency**: Open source code for community review
* **Accessibility**: Making AI technology accessible to all users
* **Education**: Promoting AI literacy and understanding
* **Collaboration**: Working together to improve AI safety and ethics

### Community Guidelines for AI Development
* **No malicious use**: Do not contribute features that could harm users
* **Respect model licenses**: Follow all third-party model licensing terms
* **Security focus**: Prioritize user security and privacy in all contributions
* **Inclusive design**: Ensure features work for diverse user populations

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

### Reporting Channels
* **GitHub Issues**: For public discussions about conduct
* **Private Contact**: For sensitive reports requiring confidentiality
* **Security Issues**: Use security@cortex-pocket.dev for security-related concerns

### Response Process
1. **Acknowledgment**: We will acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
2. **Investigation**: Thorough review of the reported issue
3. **Action**: Appropriate response based on severity and context
4. **Follow-up**: Continued monitoring to ensure resolution
